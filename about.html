About Akela

My dream is to help kids learn math and physics (and to make a living doing that).

It's going to be at least six years before I start teaching for real, but I have some things planned out already with my future students. In the middle of some physics problem, we're going to need to multiply two numbers and that's when, instead of asking the students to punch the numbers into their calculator, I'll pull out a slide rule. Maybe I'll learn how to use an abacus too. We'll definitely be doing a lot of experiments. What is the gravitational field strength? How much air resistance do different objects in free fall have?
